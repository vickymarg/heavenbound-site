JayJay’s Truth – In His Name

Justice for JayJay

Why I’m Speaking

Kayle Smith is the catalyst.

JayJay was headbutted and moments later attacked. I watched as the truth was twisted.

I speak because the lies must stop. This is for my son, and no one will silence me again.

Kayle’s mother has tried to rewrite the facts. But truth does not bend to protect the guilty.

I held JayJay’s hand for only a moment after they put him in a coma. I wasn’t there when he was asking for me. That guilt never fades.

This is my son’s truth — and I will honour it with every breath I have left.
